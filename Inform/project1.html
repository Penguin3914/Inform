Bassoons

Bassoons are double reed instruments.
They play mostly in ensembles and orchestras. Most school bands have bassoons also.


Types

There are two types of bassoons:


Normal Bassoons Contrabassoons

Parts

A normal bassoon has 5 parts:
  • Boot
  • Bell
  • Long Joint (Bass Joint)
  • Short Joint (Wing Joint)
  • Bocal (Mouthpiece)
The Contrabassoon has 6 parts:
  • Bocal
  • Wing Joint
  • Middle Joint
  • Metal Shank
  • Long Joint
  • Bell

Reeds

Reeds are neccesary to play the Bassoon. They are separate from the instrument and located on the end of the bocal
A typical price range is from $25 to $50, depending on the maker and quality.
Sound is made when the reeds vibrate against each other and the sound waves travel through the instrument.
